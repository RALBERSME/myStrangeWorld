The unbelievable happened.

Lata won the marathon. She received tremendous applause. No one had expected such a feat from her grandmother. She used the prize money of 5,000 Indian rupees for her husband's medical treatment.

This running competition in 2013 was her first marathon. Since then, she has regularly participated in marathons and used her winnings for her family's needs.

Her story was immortalized in the Marathi film "Lata Bhagwan Kare," which won an award at the National Film Awards.
